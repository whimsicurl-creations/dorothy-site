Artist Statement

My work focuses on candid photographs taken on location at events for women's rights, places where women are owning their space, and fleeting encounters on the streets. My work engulfs the viewer in real moments of strength, vulnerability, solidarity.

Quote about Work

"I capture the realness behind feminism, no filter."
